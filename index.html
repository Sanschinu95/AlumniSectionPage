About the Event

Alumni Reunion is a jubilant event aimed at celebrating the imperial legacy of graduated pupils of Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology. This annual conference will consist of diverse insightful activities and interactive sessions from accomplished alums contributing conscientiously in the field of entrepreneurship and technological advancements. As a part of our upcoming megafest, E-Summit'23, our team will be organizing an Alumni Meet to reminisce the yesteryears and share their insights from the corporate world.
